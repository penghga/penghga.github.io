About

I grew up in a Jining, a small town in the Shandong province, which is located in central eastern China. When I was thirteen, My father and I moved to Athens, GA, following my mother who was pursuing her Ph.D. degree at the University of Georgia at the time. I have since then lived in Athens, completing middle school, high school, college, and Ph.D. I also have a little brother who is fourteen years younger than me. He currently lives with our parents in Virginia.
